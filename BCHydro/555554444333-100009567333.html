Power factor is a measure of efficiency and is the ratio of usable power (kW) to reactive power (kVar) in a circuit. It varies between 0 and 1, and is normally given as a percentage (1 to 100 per cent). We apply a power factor surcharge to business customers whose power factor drops below 90 per cent.
Learn how to avoid this surcharge on bchydro.com/powerfactor.
