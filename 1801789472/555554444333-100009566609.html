Based on Residential Conservation Rate 1101
You're on the Residential Conservation Rate (Rate Schedule 1101). This means you're charged one rate for electricity up to a threshold (calculated by 22.1918 kWh per day multiplied by the number of days in the billing period), and a higher rate for all electricity beyond that threshold.
This rate structure is designed to encourage conservation and is referred to as a "stepped rate". The first portion is called Step 1 and the amount above that is called Step 2. Learn more at bchydro.com/rates.
